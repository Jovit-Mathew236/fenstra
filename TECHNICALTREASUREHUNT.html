TECHNICAL TREASURE HUNT

Treasure hunts are information-based scavenger hunts, or puzzle hunts, in which teams use their collective brainpower to solve clues. Each team is presented with a group of technical puzzles, which teams must solve in order to find a piece of information to get a final solution at the end of the hunt.
There will be 3 rounds:
1. Clue Mining -Digging files and folders to get pieces of clues/algorithm to solve the problem.
2. Puzzle
3. Expect the Unexpected- Code the collected algorithm using C/C++ without compilation.
